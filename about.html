Ryan Miemczyk

Director

Ryan has worked within the Health and Social Care sector for over 17 years and has a proven track record of delivering outcomes at both strategic and operational levels in complex political and multi-stakeholder environments.

Ryan's experience has been gained across the public, private, and charity sectors, with his current role as the Director of Research and Innovation at Trust Impact, where he has guided clients on developing strategy for impact, and building national and international impact measurement frameworks for clients such as Help for Heroes, the Felix Project, MND Association, the Institute of Imagination, Cadent GAS, and Shell UK.

Prior to this role, Ryan spent 8 years at the NSPCC, where he led projects for the prevention of sexual abuse from service development through to implementation and evaluation. Since the completion of his MSc in Forensic Psychology in 2009, Ryan has held positions as a Research Manager for the Scottish Government, before moving into psychological assessment and service development.

During Ryan's career, he has also gained qualifications in project management, operational excellence, forensic risk assessment, psychometric testing, and is a government-accredited researcher. In his spare time, Ryan also sits as the Chair of Trustees for the Manchester Young Lives charity, is a Trustee for the Manchester YMCA, and represents England at Touch Rugby.
